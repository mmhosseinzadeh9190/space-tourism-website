spaceport

A spaceport or cosmodrome is a place for launching (or receiving) spacecraft, analogous to a seaport for ships or an airport for airplanes. Our spacecraft, famously at Cape Canaveral, has the ideal climate and location to take advantage of Earth's rotation for launch.
